Colleges Of Manufacturing Excellence (COME)

by Dhairya Bhatt.

The article is part of my summer vacation homework provided by my school for subject "Social" Hope you enjoy.

Come to COME

My proposal for improving the future for young people in India is the establishment of colleges of manufacturing excellence.

There colleges will be fully funded by the central government and strictly operated by the successful international manufacturing companies.

Companies such as BMW, Volkswagen, Apple, Tesla, Astra Zeneca, would be incentivised to establish these colleges of excellence. In these colleges young people can enroll, based strictly upon a test designed by these international companies.

Students will learn how to design and develop products to the highest standard, that should be world leading quality.

Central government should fully fund these colleges as eventually this exceptionally high quality of education training should enable to be a world leader across all manufacturing sectors. Very importantly, access to these colleges would be after the completion of 10 standard.

This change will enable India to get past its reputation for producing poor quality goods.

This would also motivate students to work hard to get into their favourite company school. And to understand the real connection between school and real life.
